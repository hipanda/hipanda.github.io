Radario

Radario — a service producing and selling e-tickets to any kind of events.

Project site: radario.ru

I worked on this project as a product designer. My part was in creating the vision, designing UX and interactions, writing the copy, designing graphics from the very begining of the project.

It is a complex product with several entities. Main site — B2C e-ticket office. iOS and Android apps. Social network apps. Management system for event hosts. Ticket control system which includes interactions with addition control devices and special host-apps for Android and Windows.
