Pulseture

Pulseture — project management system that gives you a detailed control on any task in one hand and in the other hand you have a clear birdview on the entire project in any moment.

It was designed completely from napkin sketches by me and Anton Bryantsev, the ideas shipped in this system came from our past projects and the lack of one reliable product that could ease our work.
